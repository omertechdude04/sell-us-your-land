About Miry Hoffman

Miry Hoffman began her career as an elementary school educator. After the birth of her youngest son, she was diagnosed with lymphoma cancer—a turning point that inspired her to study law.

Against the odds, she overcame the disease and simultaneously became a licensed attorney. Miry founded a successful law firm, practicing real estate law for many years.

Over time, she evolved into a real estate entrepreneur, initially investing in houses and later shifting her focus to land transactions, construction, and global real estate investments.

Today, Miry is a successful investor, collaborating with partners across the U.S. and internationally. Her personal journey of resilience and reinvention is extraordinary—she is currently writing a book to inspire others with her story.
